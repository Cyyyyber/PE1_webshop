As a visual artist I'm deeply captivated by the Renaissance era, a time of artistic rebirth and intellectual awakening. My work is a homage to this period's profound influence on art and culture. I channel their fascination into a passionate exploration of the human body, mirroring the Renaissance masters' meticulous attention to anatomy and form. Through my art, I seek to uncover the timeless beauty and complexity of the human physique, bridging the gap between the past and present with a contemporary perspective that pays homage to the rich heritage of the Renaissance.
